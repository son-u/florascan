About Us

Introducing FloraScan

FloraScan is an advanced flower recognition system employing state-of-the-art machine learning and image analysis. Our user-friendly platform swiftly identifies diverse floral species, providing comprehensive information with just an uploaded image or snapshot. Passionate about botany and technology, our team aims to simplify flower identification for enthusiasts and professionals alike, fostering a deeper understanding and appreciation of nature's beauty.
